01 Overview

My research explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, systems theory, and thermodynamic constraint. I examine how entropy management, integrity, and memory fidelity form structural invariants for long-term cooperative intelligence.

Current work includes Integrity-Based Alignment (IBA), The Maintenance Economy, and Project Anamnesis. I also spend time modeling economic behavior as part of broader system dynamics, with a practical and exploratory lens.
